Who We Are

About Organization

Rejuveta Heritage & Conservation is a new heritage care organization committed to preserving cultural identity through documentation, research, awareness, and community participation.

We work to make heritage more visible, understandable, and protected so future generations can inherit living knowledge, not just old structures.
